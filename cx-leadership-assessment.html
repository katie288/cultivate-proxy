Before we change anything, we need to see clearly. This assessment is your honest starting point — not a test, not a judgement. A mirror.

What this assessment covers
01

Organisation & Customer Context

Who you serve, how you serve them, and the business landscape you're operating in.

02

Your Leadership Position

Your role, influence, resource, professional development, and personal leadership.

03

CX Maturity

How developed your organisation's approach to CX actually is — beyond the narrative.

04

Cultural Conditions

The hidden forces quietly shaping the experience your customers receive.

05

Customer Understanding

How deeply your organisation truly knows its customers — emotionally and behaviourally.

06

Strategy & Governance

Whether CX has a clear strategic home and how accountability is structured.

07

Measurement & Meaning

What you measure, why, and whether it's informing better decisions.

08

Employee Experience

The connection between how your people are led and how customers are treated.

09

Technology & AI

How technology and AI are shaping your CX — and whether that's intentional.

10

Your Honest Reflection

Space to articulate what you see, feel, and believe — in your own words.

There are no right or wrong answers. The value of this assessment is entirely dependent on your honesty. Answer as things are — not as you wish they were. This report belongs to you.
